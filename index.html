NMIMS (Narsee Monjee Institute of Management Studies) is a leading private university located in Mumbai, India. Established in 1981, it offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in various fields such as Management, Engineering, Commerce, Law, and Pharmacy, among others. NMIMS has been ranked among the top 20 management institutes in India and is known for its academic rigor, industry-oriented curriculum, and emphasis on research and innovation. The university has a diverse student community from across India and other countries and provides a vibrant and conducive environment for overall personal and professional development.
